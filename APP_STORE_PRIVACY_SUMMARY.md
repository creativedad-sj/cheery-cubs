# App Store Privacy Summary for Cheery Cubs

## Summary

Cheery Cubs does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information from children or parents through the app.

## Data Collection

Cheery Cubs does not collect personal data such as:

- name
- email address
- phone number
- location
- contacts
- photos
- payment details

## Data Stored on Device

The app stores limited information locally on the device to provide functionality, including:

- game progress
- earned stickers
- app settings
- screen time settings
- parent PIN

This data is stored locally and is not sent to our servers.

## Tracking

Cheery Cubs does not use advertising trackers, analytics trackers, or custom profiling tools in the current version of the app.

## Login and Accounts

Cheery Cubs does not require login or account creation.
