# Privacy Policy for Cheery Cubs

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Cheery Cubs is designed for young children and their parents. We aim to keep the app simple, private, and safe.

## Information We Collect

Cheery Cubs does not require an account or login.

Cheery Cubs does not collect personal information such as:

- name
- email address
- phone number
- location
- photos
- contacts
- payment information

## Information Stored on Your Device

Cheery Cubs stores some app data locally on your device so the app can work properly. This may include:

- game progress
- earned stickers and rewards
- app settings
- question mode preferences
- screen time settings
- parent PIN

This information stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

## Internet and Data Sharing

Cheery Cubs does not send your data to our servers.

Cheery Cubs does not use:

- user accounts
- cloud sync
- in-app chat
- advertising SDKs
- analytics SDKs
- custom tracking tools

If you use the app during development through Expo Go, some development tooling may connect to Expo services to load the app while testing. This is only part of development and is not part of the normal released app experience.

## Children’s Privacy

Cheery Cubs is intended for young children with parent involvement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services

Cheery Cubs uses device features such as:

- text-to-speech for spoken prompts
- vibration or haptic feedback

These features are used only to provide app functionality on the device.

## Data Security

Because Cheery Cubs stores app data locally on the device, protecting the device itself is important. Parents should use normal device security practices such as passcodes and device updates.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update the date at the top of this policy.
